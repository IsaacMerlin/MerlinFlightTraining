Services Page

Flight Instruction

Learn the basics and advanced skills needed to become a safe and confident pilot.

Private Pilot

Earn your license to fly your own plane or others planes with your private pilot license. You can earn it in as little as 40 hours of flight time. Merlin Flight Training's average cost is $23,000

Instrument Rating

Earn your instrument rating to fly in the clouds and in low visibility conditions. The Instrument rating can be acquired in as little as 40 hours of flight time. The average cost of instrument rating is $18,000

Commercial Pilot

Earn your commercial pilot license to fly for hire, you will need to complete at least 250 hours of flight time in order to get this certificate. We at Merlin Flight Training provide the instruction needed to receive your commercial pilot license within only 25 hours of flight training. Often times students are able to complete this certificate in even less time. The typical cost of training for only 20 hours is $4,000

Flight Review

Every 24 months you are required to complete a flight review with a certified flight instructor. This is a great way to keep your skills sharp and stay current. The cost of a flight review is $300. This includes 1 hour of flight training and 1 hour of ground.

High Performance Endorsement

Earn your high performance endorsement to fly high performance aircraft. This endorsement is required to fly any aircraft with more than 200 horsepower. The cost of this endorsement is $500 and includes 1 hour of flight training and 1 hour of ground.

About Merlin Flight Training

Merlin Flight Training offers top-notch flight training services to aspiring pilots. Our experienced instructors and comprehensive programs ensure that you receive the best training possible.
