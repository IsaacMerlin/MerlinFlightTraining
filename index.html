About Us

Merlin Flight Training provides aviation enthusiasts with the necessary training to become the pilot of their dreams. Located in the New Jersey and New York area, we are here to provide an excellent service for you. Our owner, Isaac, is a Certified Flight Instructor with a passion for teaching. With experience in high-altitude and mountain flying from Utah Valley, Isaac has worked as a pesticide pilot, operating multi-engine aircraft at low levels during night operations. Now, as a Certified Flight Instructor with an Instrument Instructor add-on, he proudly serves the NY and NJ area with his enthusiasm for teaching.
