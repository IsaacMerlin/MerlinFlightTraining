High Performance Endorsement

Expand your flying privileges! Learn about the requirements, training, costs, and benefits of earning your High Performance Endorsement with Merlin Flight Training.

What is a High Performance Endorsement?

A High Performance Endorsement is required by the FAA to act as pilot in command of any aircraft with an engine of more than 200 horsepower. This endorsement demonstrates your ability to safely operate more powerful and capable airplanes.

Requirements

  • Hold at least a Private Pilot Certificate
  • Receive and log ground and flight training from an authorized instructor in a high performance airplane
  • Demonstrate proficiency and understanding of high performance aircraft systems and operations

Training Overview

  • Review of high performance aircraft systems and procedures
  • Flight training in a high performance airplane
  • Focus on power management, performance, and safety
  • Completion of required maneuvers and procedures

Cost

The cost for a High Performance Endorsement at Merlin Flight Training is $500. This includes 1 hour of flight training and 1 hour of ground instruction.

Why Get a High Performance Endorsement?

  • Fly aircraft with more than 200 horsepower
  • Increase your skill and confidence as a pilot
  • Access a wider range of rental and owned aircraft
  • Build experience for advanced ratings and career opportunities
