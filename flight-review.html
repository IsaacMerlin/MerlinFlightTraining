Flight Review

Stay current and safe! Learn about the flight review process, requirements, costs, and how Merlin Flight Training can help you stay proficient and legal to fly.

What is a Flight Review?

A Flight Review (formerly known as a Biennial Flight Review or BFR) is required by the FAA every 24 months for all pilots who wish to act as pilot in command. It is not a test, but an opportunity to refresh your knowledge and skills with a certified flight instructor.

Requirements

  • Must be completed every 24 calendar months
  • Consists of at least 1 hour of ground instruction
  • Includes at least 1 hour of flight training
  • Review of current regulations and safe operating practices

What to Expect

  • Personalized review based on your experience and flying habits
  • Discussion of recent changes in regulations and procedures
  • Practice of maneuvers and procedures as needed
  • Opportunity to ask questions and improve your skills

Cost

The cost for a Flight Review at Merlin Flight Training is $300. This includes 1 hour of ground instruction and 1 hour of flight training.
